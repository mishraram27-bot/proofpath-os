# Proof Submission Template

## Title

Short descriptive title of your proof

## Context

What problem or concept are you working on?

## Your Thinking

Explain your reasoning clearly.

## Decision

What did you decide and why?

## Improvement

What did you improve compared to current state?

## Impact

How does this help in real-world use?

## Proof Type

- Product breakdown
- System design
- UX improvement
- AI logic
- Business decision
- Code / technical logic

## Rule

Proof must show thinking + decision + improvement.
