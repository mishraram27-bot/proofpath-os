# Exam Template

## Section 1 — Concept (20%)

Explain the concept clearly in simple terms.

## Section 2 — Breakdown (30%)

Analyze a real product or system.

## Section 3 — System Design (30%)

Design a workflow or system.

## Section 4 — Decision (20%)

Explain what you would NOT build and why.

## Passing Criteria

70% or above = pass

Below 70% = repeat and improve
