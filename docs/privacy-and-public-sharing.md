# Privacy and Public Sharing

ProofPath OS is designed for public proof and learning, but privacy must be protected.

## Rule

Public proof should show thinking, not expose private data.

## Do NOT share

- client names (without permission)
- confidential business data
- private conversations
- API keys or credentials
- proprietary internal systems

## Safe Sharing

Use:

- anonymized examples
- generic company names
- redacted screenshots
- simplified system diagrams

## Portfolio Use

Proofs can be used in portfolio if:

- they show thinking
- they avoid sensitive data
- they focus on decisions and improvements

## Responsibility

You are responsible for what you publish.

If in doubt, remove or anonymize.
