# Exam Rubric

The exam system prevents weak self-passing and protects the quality of progression.

## Exam Sections

| Section | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Concept explanation | 20% |
| Product or system breakdown | 30% |
| System design | 30% |
| Decision judgment | 20% |

## Passing Rule

To pass an exam:

- total score must be 70% or higher
- no section can score below 50%

If either condition fails, the exam is not passed.

## Section Standards

### 1. Concept Explanation

A strong answer explains the idea simply, accurately, and with an example.

### 2. Breakdown

A strong answer identifies the real problem, trade-offs, user impact, and business impact.

### 3. System Design

A strong answer shows flow, dependencies, failure points, and improvement logic.

### 4. Decision Judgment

A strong answer explains what should not be built, delayed, removed, or simplified.

## Retake Rule

If failed:

1. identify weak sections
2. complete repair sessions
3. produce one proof for each weak section
4. retake only after repair proof is complete

## Rule

No exam pass, no phase unlock.
